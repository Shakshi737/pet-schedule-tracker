Vaccine Schedule

Keep track of important vaccinations for your dog. Properly timed vaccinations are essential for your pet's health and well-being.

Puppy Vaccines (0-6 months)

Distemper & Parvovirus (DHPP) - First Dose
Protection against highly contagious and serious diseases.
6-8 weeks
DHPP - Second Dose
Second dose in the vaccination series.
10-12 weeks
DHPP - Third Dose
Final dose in the initial vaccination series.
14-16 weeks
Rabies Vaccine
Required by law in most areas. Protects against fatal disease.
16 weeks
Bordetella (Kennel Cough)
Recommended if your dog will attend daycare, boarding, or dog parks.
8-16 weeks

Juvenile & Adult Vaccines (6 months - 7 years)

DHPP Booster
Follow-up to puppy series to maintain immunity.
1 year after puppy series
Rabies Booster
Required by law in most areas.
1-3 years (depends on vaccine type)
DHPP Booster
Regular booster to maintain immunity.
Every 1-3 years
Bordetella Booster
Recommended annually for social dogs.
Every 6-12 months
Leptospirosis
Recommended in areas where this bacterial disease is common.
Annually

Senior Dog Vaccines (7+ years)

DHPP Booster
Continued protection, may be administered less frequently based on vet assessment.
Every 1-3 years
Rabies Booster
Required by law in most areas.
Every 1-3 years
Bordetella (if socially active)
Recommended for dogs that visit groomers, kennels, or dog parks.
Every 6-12 months
Print Schedule

Important Information

  • This schedule is a general guideline. Your veterinarian may recommend a customized schedule based on your dog's specific needs.
  • Some areas have different requirements for rabies vaccination frequency.
  • Additional vaccines may be recommended based on your geographic location and your dog's lifestyle.
  • Always consult with your veterinarian before starting any vaccination protocol.
